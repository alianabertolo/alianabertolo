Hello there, My name is Aliana and I just entered my third decade of life! I was born in Ecuador and lived there for 18 years, the next 6 years I moved around Europe until I settled in The Netherlands in 2014. Living in lots of different countries is bliss and a curse at the same time, since many people I like do not live in a biking radius, but also, it made me a very empathic human, especially related to different cultures and ways of thinking.

 

I have been working on projects surrounding design, sustainability, and emotions for over 5 years. I like experimenting with design and figuring out the best solution for a specific problem. I also really enjoy visualizing data in ways that are easier to digest, in any medium available, but especially graphic design, animations, and d3.

 

I am a weird mixture between being curious, and all-over creative whilst being extremely methodical, organized, and reflective. Say hello here.
