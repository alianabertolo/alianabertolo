Tiny Houses Enthusiasts

Tiny Houses Enthusiasts is an Online platform that provides the possibility of exchanging information and creating a bridge between people that have in common the tiny house lifestyle.

Watch

video

here

The Tiny House Movement is an architectural and social movement that encourages living a simpler life in a smaller space. By living in a Tiny House, you create room in your life for building relationships, spend time in nature and focus on having experiences rather than possessions.

 

The Online platform works community based, once you search for the specific community you see a map with the facilities available in that specific community for instance: different density areas of settlement, dog parks, community gardens, laundry facilities, meeting rooms, pool and picnic area.

 

The platform also serves as a place to find the latest news regarding the movement worldwide, as well as a collaborative guide where members of the community can write articles or post vlogs about their experiences during planning, construction and living. Lastly, the platform contains an area reserved for petitions, where signatures are collected to support local laws that facilitate the creation of more communities, as well as donations for the ownership of the land where the community stands. For every donation, you receive a little tiny house made with recycled cardboard that you assemble yourself :)
