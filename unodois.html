Loneliness is an emotional response to isolation or lack of companionship. Loneliness includes anxious feelings about lack of connection of communication with other beings. Unodois is a collection of  daily life objects made out of ceramics. Normally used by one person, these objects were revived to be used by two, creating bonds between individuals.

Unodois

 

Each piece of the collection was created by cast slipping ceramics into plaster molds.
