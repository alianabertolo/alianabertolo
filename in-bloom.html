In bloom

In bloom is a Pop-up store designed for an immerse, experiential and interactive experience with flowers, sponsored by 12 plants and flowers growers from The Netherlands.

This event was created in collaboration of International Communication Management and Industrial Design Engineering students from The Hague University of Applied Sciences.
