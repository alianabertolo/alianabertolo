Hydroponics is a method of growing plants without soil. The main objective of HEXAponics is to provide the possibility of growing a -do it yourself- herb garden at home. A variety of herbs were selected and grouped in other to make 3 sets consisting of Italian, Indian, and Proven√ßal flavors.

The main part of this product is 4 part structure which assembles into the container together with an hexagonal base, where the herbs are grown. The product works on passive, non-circulating hydroponic system, which is easy to use even by inexperienced growers. You can hang or place your system anywhere in the house and create your own indoor garden.
